Life in the Coding House

Coding House is a 60 day live on site coding bootcamp and an experience like none other. Going into Coding House I expected 60 days of nonstop code but it is much more than that. There are three main aspects to Coding House: the code, the social aspects, and the relationships.

As you might expect, coding takes up most of the day. The mornings are all about learning the code and the afternoons are all about living the code. The first thing that you will learn at Coding House is that if you think you already know that days topic, you're wrong. Learning at the Coding House is nothing like learning in a typical classroom. Your class room is your home and you will learn throughout it. Your classmates are your roommates and they will always push to take the extra step. Your instructor is brilliant and will lean over your shoulder to teach you until you understand every aspect. Coding House is more than just code, its also about having a social presence online.

Before Coding House I thought that all I needed to do to get a great job was be really good at writing code, but this is far from the truth. Your life changing code won't change anyone's life if nobody knows that it exists. Being active on social networks and having a social presence is just as important as writing great code. Coding House will teach that you need so many LinkedIn connections that they won't even tell you how many you have. Your github profile needs to be all green, dark green. Your twitter account should be always buzzing with the latest technology tweets and gaining followers daily. If your wondering what a good Klout score is, it's 100. However, personal relationships are even more important than your virtual ones.

Coding House gives you the opportunity to create and grow vital life long relationships with some amazing people. You're roommates at Coding House are not only your classmates and your friends, but they are people that will help you grow throughout your professional career. Coding House will also give you the opportunity to meet and connect with industry professionals, people who are masters of their domain. These relationships have the potential to take you further in your career than you thought possible and are absolutely crucial to your success.
